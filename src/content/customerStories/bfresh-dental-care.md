problem: "Sebelum proses digitalisasi, sebagian besar aktivitas di Bfresh Dental Care masih berjalan secara offline dan terpisah. Pencatatan rekam medis, laporan omzet, data pasien, hingga reservasi dokter dilakukan secara manual atau menggunakan sistem yang berbeda-beda. Hal ini meningkatkan risiko kesalahan pencatatan, menyulitkan pelacakan histori pasien, serta membuat proses operasional menjadi tidak efisien. Di sisi lain, pasien juga membutuhkan kemudahan dalam mengakses informasi klinik, melakukan reservasi, dan mengetahui promo tanpa harus datang langsung atau menghubungi klinik secara manual."
problemEn: "Before the digitalization process, most activities at Bfresh Dental Care were still running offline and separately. Recording of medical records, revenue reports, patient data, and doctor reservations were done manually or using different systems. This increased the risk of recording errors, made it difficult to track patient history, and made operational processes inefficient. On the other hand, patients also needed convenience in accessing clinic information, making reservations, and knowing about promotions without having to come directly or contact the clinic manually."
solution: "Dental360 Advanced menjadi solusi terintegrasi yang mengonlinekan seluruh aktivitas Bfresh Dental Care dalam satu platform. Selain mendukung operasional internal yang lebih rapi dan efisien, Dental360 juga memungkinkan klinik untuk berinteraksi langsung dengan pasien melalui update promo dan sistem reservasi yang lebih praktis. Hasilnya, operasional klinik berjalan lebih terkontrol, data terdokumentasi dengan baik, dan pengalaman pasien meningkat secara signifikan."
solutionEn: "Dental360 Advanced became an integrated solution that brought all Bfresh Dental Care activities online in one platform. This system includes revenue report management, patient statistics, digital medical records, to online doctor reservations that can be adjusted according to date and doctor availability. In addition to supporting more organized and efficient internal operations, Dental360 also allows the clinic to interact directly with patients through promotional updates and a more practical reservation system. As a result, clinic operations run more controlled, data is well documented, and patient experience significantly improved."
