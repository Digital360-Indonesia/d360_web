overviewEn: "Terminal Cargo Indonesia is a logistics company that serves goods delivery with various route options and shipping schemes. The company serves customer needs both internally and externally, from checking shipping prices to tracking goods status. With a wide service coverage, the company needed a digital system capable of connecting internal operational needs and ease of access for customers in one platform."
problem: "Sebelum digitalisasi, proses pencatatan dan penyampaian informasi di Terminal Cargo Indonesia masih dilakukan secara manual dan terpisah. Informasi harga pengiriman, jenis layanan, serta status pengiriman belum terintegrasi dalam satu sistem yang mudah diakses oleh pelanggan. Di sisi internal, pencatatan transaksi dan data pengiriman membutuhkan proses administratif tambahan yang memakan waktu. Kondisi ini berpotensi menurunkan efisiensi operasional dan pengalaman pelanggan."
problemEn: "Before digitalization, the recording and information delivery process at Terminal Cargo Indonesia was still done manually and separately. Shipping price information, service types, and shipping status were not yet integrated in one system that was easily accessible by customers. On the internal side, transaction recording and shipping data required additional time-consuming administrative processes. This condition had the potential to reduce operational efficiency and customer experience."
solution: "Terminal Cargo Indonesia mengimplementasikan dashboard digital yang dapat digunakan baik oleh tim internal maupun pelanggan. Sistem ini memungkinkan pelanggan untuk mengecek harga pengiriman berdasarkan berat dan tujuan kota, memilih jenis layanan seperti port to port, pengiriman laut, atau trucking, serta melakukan tracking atau cek resi secara mandiri. Sementara itu, tim internal dapat melakukan pencatatan data pengiriman secara terpusat. Dengan digitalisasi ini, alur kerja menjadi lebih efisien, informasi lebih transparan, dan pengalaman pelanggan meningkat secara signifikan."
solutionEn: "Terminal Cargo Indonesia implemented a digital dashboard that can be used by both internal teams and customers. This system allows customers to check shipping prices based on weight and destination city, choose service types such as port to port, sea shipping, or trucking, and perform tracking or check receipts independently. Meanwhile, the internal team can record shipping data centrally. With this digitalization, workflow becomes more efficient, information more transparent, and customer experience significantly improved."
