overviewEn: "Vido Garment is a manufacturing company engaged in apparel and uniform production with workflows involving many divisions, from production, sales, marketing, procurement, finance, to HR. With complex and project-based production processes, Vido Garment needed an integrated system capable of connecting all operational activities in one platform. The goal was to ensure every division could work synchronously, data-driven, and support faster and more measurable business decision-making."
problem: "Sebelum pengembangan dashboard ERP terintegrasi, aktivitas operasional di Vido Garment berjalan secara terpisah antar divisi. Tim marketing, sales, finance, dan procurement tidak berada dalam satu sistem yang sama dengan tim produksi, sehingga data dan komunikasi sering kali tersebar di berbagai tools dan platform. Kondisi ini menyulitkan manajemen untuk mendapatkan gambaran real-time terkait progres produksi, status proyek, ketersediaan bahan, hingga performa penjualan. Kurangnya satu pintu data juga meningkatkan risiko miskomunikasi, keterlambatan informasi, dan ketidaksinkronan antar divisi dalam menjalankan proyek produksi."
problemEn: "Before the development of integrated ERP dashboard, operational activities at Vido Garment ran separately between divisions. Marketing, sales, finance, and procurement teams were not in the same system as the production team, so data and communication were often scattered across various tools and platforms. This condition made it difficult for management to get a real-time picture of production progress, project status, material availability, to sales performance. The lack of a single data gateway also increased the risk of miscommunication, information delays, and lack of synchronization between divisions in running production projects."
solution: "Vido Garment mengimplementasikan dashboard ERP advance yang dirancang khusus untuk menjawab kebutuhan setiap divisi dalam satu ekosistem terintegrasi. Sistem ini mencakup tahapan produksi end-to-end, mulai dari desain, pembelian bahan, proses potong, bordir atau sablon, jahit, finishing, quality control, hingga pengiriman, lengkap dengan laporan jumlah produksi per jenis item setiap bulan. Di sisi lain, tim sales dapat mencatat prospek, aktivitas follow-up, dan kebutuhan invoice, sementara tim marketing memantau sponsorship serta laporan media sosial yang terhubung dengan data prospek. Dashboard ini juga mendukung pencatatan keuangan yang komprehensif untuk tim finance, absensi online dan database karyawan untuk tim HR, serta pengelolaan proyek secara terpusat. Hasilnya, seluruh divisi Vido Garment kini terhubung dalam satu sistem, data lebih rapi, dan operasional berjalan lebih efisien serta terkontrol."
solutionEn: "Vido Garment implemented an advanced ERP dashboard specifically designed to meet the needs of each division in one integrated ecosystem. This system includes end-to-end production stages, from design, material purchasing, cutting process, embroidery or screen printing, sewing, finishing, quality control, to delivery, complete with monthly production quantity reports per item type. On the other hand, the sales team can record prospects, follow-up activities, and invoice requirements, while the marketing team monitors sponsorship and social media reports connected to prospect data. This dashboard also supports comprehensive financial recording for the finance team, online attendance and employee database for the HR team, as well as centralized project management. As a result, all Vido Garment divisions are now connected in one system, data is more organized, and operations run more efficiently and controlled."
