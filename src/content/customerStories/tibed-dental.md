Fokus utama manajemen adalah memahami perkembangan omzet dan karakteristik pasien secara lebih akurat agar keputusan bisnis dapat diambil dengan cepat dan berbasis data, bukan sekadar perkiraan."
overviewEn: "Tibed Dental is a dental clinic that serves patients with various types of treatments, from basic procedures to advanced care. As the number of patients and treatment variations increased, Tibed Dental needed a system capable of providing a comprehensive overview of clinic performance in real-time. Management's main focus was to understand revenue development and patient characteristics more accurately so that business decisions could be made quickly and data-driven, rather than just estimates."
problem: "Sebelum menggunakan sistem terpusat, proses pelaporan di Tibed Dental masih dilakukan secara manual dan terpisah. Data omzet per treatment, total pendapatan, serta statistik pasien tersimpan di berbagai sumber berbeda, sehingga sulit untuk dianalisis secara menyeluruh. Kondisi ini membuat manajemen membutuhkan waktu lebih lama untuk mengevaluasi performa klinik, membandingkan treatment yang paling berkontribusi, serta memahami profil pasien yang datang. Akibatnya, pengambilan keputusan strategis seperti penentuan fokus layanan atau evaluasi kinerja klinik menjadi kurang optimal dan berisiko tidak tepat sasaran."
problemEn: "Before using a centralized system, the reporting process at Tibed Dental was still done manually and separately. Revenue data per treatment, total income, and patient statistics were stored in various different sources, making it difficult to analyze comprehensively. This situation caused management to need more time to evaluate clinic performance, compare the most contributing treatments, and understand the profile of incoming patients. As a result, strategic decision-making such as determining service focus or evaluating clinic performance became less optimal and risked being off-target."
solution: "Dental360 hadir sebagai dashboard laporan terpusat yang membantu Tibed Dental memantau performa klinik secara menyeluruh dalam satu tampilan. Melalui fitur laporan omzet by treatment, total omzet, serta statistik pasien berbasis demografi, manajemen dapat langsung melihat data penting secara real-time. Informasi yang sebelumnya tersebar kini tersaji dalam bentuk insight yang mudah dipahami, sehingga klinik dapat mengidentifikasi layanan unggulan, membaca tren pasien, dan mengambil keputusan bisnis dengan lebih cepat, akurat, dan berbasis data."
solutionEn: "Dental360 emerged as a centralized reporting dashboard that helps Tibed Dental monitor clinic performance comprehensively in one view. Through features such as revenue reports by treatment, total revenue, and demographic-based patient statistics, management can directly view important data in real-time. Information that was previously scattered is now presented in easy-to-understand insights, enabling the clinic to identify leading services, read patient trends, and make business decisions faster, more accurately, and data-driven."
