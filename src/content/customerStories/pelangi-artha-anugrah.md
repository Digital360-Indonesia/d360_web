overview: "PT Pelangi Artha Anugrah merupakan perusahaan outsourcing yang berfokus pada pengelolaan sumber daya manusia sebagai inti bisnisnya. Aktivitas perusahaan mencakup proses end-to-end pengelolaan tenaga kerja, mulai dari rekrutmen, administrasi karyawan, hingga pengelolaan hak dan kewajiban karyawan setelah diterima bekerja. Dengan jumlah data karyawan yang terus bertambah, perusahaan membutuhkan sistem yang mampu mendukung pengelolaan people management secara terstruktur, rapi, dan terdokumentasi dengan baik."
overviewEn: "PT Pelangi Artha Anugrah is an outsourcing company that focuses on human resource management as its core business. The company's activities include end-to-end workforce management processes, from recruitment, employee administration, to managing employee rights and obligations after being hired. With the continuously growing number of employee data, the company needed a system capable of supporting people management in a structured, organized, and well-documented manner."
problem: "Sebelum implementasi sistem digital, PT Pelangi Artha Anugrah masih bergantung pada dokumen fisik dan pencatatan manual untuk mengelola data karyawan. Arsip rekrutmen, absensi, penggajian, bonus, hingga data BPJS tersebar dalam berbagai bentuk dokumen kertas dan file terpisah. Kondisi ini menyulitkan tim HR dalam melakukan pencarian data, meningkatkan risiko kehilangan dokumen, serta memperlambat proses administrasi. Selain itu, keterbatasan sistem arsip manual membuat monitoring dan pelaporan data karyawan menjadi kurang efisien dan tidak real-time."
problemEn: "Before implementing a digital system, PT Pelangi Artha Anugrah still relied on physical documents and manual recording to manage employee data. Recruitment archives, attendance, payroll, bonuses, and BPJS data were scattered in various forms of paper documents and separate files. This situation made it difficult for the HR team to search for data, increased the risk of document loss, and slowed down administrative processes. In addition, the limitations of the manual archiving system made monitoring and reporting employee data less efficient and not real-time."
solution: "Perusahaan mengimplementasikan dashboard HR terintegrasi yang mengonlinekan seluruh kebutuhan pengelolaan sumber daya manusia dalam satu platform. Sistem ini mencakup proses rekrutmen, database karyawan, absensi online, penggajian, pengelolaan bonus, hingga pencatatan BPJS dan administrasi lainnya. Seluruh data karyawan kini terdokumentasi secara digital, terpusat, dan mudah diakses sesuai kebutuhan. Dengan sistem ini, tim HR dapat bekerja lebih efisien, proses administrasi menjadi lebih cepat, dan risiko kehilangan arsip dapat diminimalkan secara signifikan."
solutionEn: "The company implemented an integrated HR dashboard that brought all human resource management needs online in one platform. This system includes recruitment processes, employee database, online attendance, payroll, bonus management, to BPJS recording and other administration. All employee data is now digitally documented, centralized, and easily accessible as needed. With this system, the HR team can work more efficiently, administrative processes become faster, and the risk of losing archives can be significantly minimized."
