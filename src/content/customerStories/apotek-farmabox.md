overview: "Farmabox adalah apotek yang melayani kebutuhan obat dan produk kesehatan masyarakat, sekaligus menjalankan program tambahan seperti cek kesehatan gratis bagi pelanggan. Dengan volume transaksi harian dan ragam data yang terus bertambah, Farmabox membutuhkan sistem yang mampu mengelola operasional apotek secara menyeluruh. Fokus utama manajemen adalah memastikan pencatatan penjualan, data pasien, dan stok produk dapat dikelola secara terpusat agar operasional berjalan lebih efisien dan terkontrol."
overviewEn: "Farmabox is a pharmacy that serves the community's medicine and health product needs, while also running additional programs such as free health check for customers. With daily transaction volume and variety of data that continues to grow, Farmabox needed a system capable of managing pharmacy operations comprehensively. Management's main focus was to ensure that sales records, patient data, and product stock could be managed centrally so that operations run more efficiently and controlled."
problem: "Sebelum menggunakan sistem digital terintegrasi, sebagian besar aktivitas operasional Farmabox masih dilakukan secara offline. Pencatatan penjualan, transaksi pembelian, data pasien dari program cek kesehatan, hingga stok produk dikelola secara manual dan terpisah. Kondisi ini menyulitkan apotek untuk memantau pergerakan stok secara akurat, membaca pola kebutuhan obat, serta menyusun laporan pembelian dan penjualan dengan cepat. Selain berisiko terjadi kesalahan pencatatan, proses stock opname juga sering mengganggu operasional karena harus menghentikan transaksi masuk dan keluar."
problemEn: "Before using an integrated digital system, most of Farmabox's operational activities were still done offline. Sales recording, purchase transactions, patient data from health check programs, and product stock were managed manually and separately. This situation made it difficult for the pharmacy to monitor stock movements accurately, read medicine demand patterns, and compile purchase and sales reports quickly. Besides the risk of recording errors, the stock opname process also often disrupted operations because it had to stop incoming and outgoing transactions."
solution: "Melalui sistem aplikasi apotek terintegrasi, Farmabox berhasil mengonlinekan seluruh aktivitas operasional dalam satu dashboard. Pencatatan penjualan dan transaksi kini tercatat otomatis, dilengkapi dengan data pasien dari program cek kesehatan. Fitur smart forecasting membantu apotek menganalisis histori produk terlaris dan memberikan rekomendasi jumlah serta jenis produk yang perlu direstock. Selain itu, laporan pembelian dapat digunakan untuk memetakan tren kebutuhan obat, sementara stock opname dapat dilakukan tanpa harus menutup transaksi. Hasilnya, operasional apotek menjadi lebih efisien, data lebih akurat, dan keputusan restock dapat dilakukan berbasis data."
solutionEn: "Through an integrated pharmacy application system, Farmabox successfully brought all operational activities online in one dashboard. Sales and transaction recording is now automatically recorded, complete with patient data from health check programs. The smart forecasting feature helps the pharmacy analyze best-selling product history and provide recommendations on the quantity and types of products that need to be restocked. In addition, purchase reports can be used to map medicine demand trends, while stock opname can be done without having to close transactions. As a result, pharmacy operations become more efficient, data more accurate, and restocking decisions can be made data-driven."
