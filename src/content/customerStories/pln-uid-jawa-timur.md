overviewEn: "PLN UID Jawa Timur is a distribution parent unit of a state-owned company that has a strategic role in managing relationships with various stakeholders. One of the important functions within it is the TJSL division which handles cooperation, social activities, and stakeholder relations. These activities generate many documents, activity data, and archives that need to be managed systematically and sustainably."
problem: "Pengelolaan dokumen dan data di divisi TJSL sebelumnya masih dilakukan secara manual dan tersebar. Arsip MoU, dokumentasi kegiatan, data peserta event, hingga database stakeholder tersimpan dalam berbagai format dan lokasi berbeda. Kondisi ini menyulitkan proses pencarian data, monitoring kegiatan, serta pelacakan histori kerja sama dengan stakeholder. Selain memakan waktu, sistem arsip manual juga meningkatkan risiko duplikasi data dan keterbatasan dalam melakukan pelaporan yang rapi dan terstruktur."
problemEn: "Document and data management in the TJSL division was previously still done manually and scattered. MoU archives, activity documentation, event participant data, and stakeholder database were stored in various different formats and locations. This situation made data search processes difficult, activity monitoring, and tracking cooperation history with stakeholders. Besides being time-consuming, the manual archiving system also increased the risk of data duplication and limitations in conducting organized and structured reporting."
solution: "PLN UID Jawa Timur mengembangkan sistem digital khusus yang dirancang sesuai kebutuhan divisi TJSL. Dashboard ini mengintegrasikan pencatatan dokumen MoU, data kegiatan event lengkap dengan peserta dan dokumentasi foto, serta database stakeholder yang digunakan untuk keperluan relationship building dan nurturing. Seluruh arsip kini terdigitalisasi dan tersimpan dalam satu sistem terpusat, sehingga memudahkan pelacakan data, penyusunan laporan, serta pengelolaan hubungan jangka panjang dengan stakeholder secara lebih terstruktur dan profesional."
solutionEn: "PLN UID Jawa Timur developed a custom digital system designed according to the needs of the TJSL division. This dashboard integrates MoU document recording, event activity data complete with participants and photo documentation, as well as a stakeholder database used for relationship building and nurturing purposes. All archives are now digitalized and stored in one centralized system, making it easier to track data, compile reports, and manage long-term relationships with stakeholders in a more structured and professional manner."
